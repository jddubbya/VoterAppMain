Before You Get Started
VoteRef.com provides on-demand public access to voter registration records collected from state and local election officials.

Our mission is to ensure fair elections by providing voters with full transparency and easy access to voting records, which historically have been difficult to obtain.

VoteRef.com is for election-related, noncommercial use and for users based in the United States only. Before accessing the site, please read our Terms of Service and agree you will abide by them by clicking on the Accept button below.
